Made with love by Connor Rowland.

Alexa has never been overly emotional…until now. Today, Alexa knows to give just the right response to emotionally charged questions.She has been trained to understand the deeper in your words, and give you the answers you deserve.

I decided to make this project in response to feeling like my words lacked impact when my friends were going through times of difficulty in their lives. I feel that my friends and I are in an age group often defined by defiance, which makes it difficult to offer advice to a friend and have them actually take it. But we all still go to each other for support and help. So I figured Alexa could help out in the meantime, and provide the answers I would have given to common questions I hear my friends/peers ask.
